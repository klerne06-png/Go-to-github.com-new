What's Shakin'

Fresh-Squeezed Goodness

What began at a 4th of July event in 2022 with just a tent and a big dream has grown into a full mobile beverage experience. Fresh-squeezed lemon shake-ups, 15+ flavor add-ins, sugar-free options, and fruit boba!

  • Fresh-squeezed lemon shake-ups & limeades
  • 15+ flavor add-ins including exotic options
  • Sugar-free flavors available
  • Fruit boba toppings
  • Cotton candy, pickles & more

Handcrafted Coffee & More

In 2025, we took another leap! Our coffee trailer brings specialty drinks like Muddy Paws and Mint Bomb, 27+ syrup flavors, and the full coffeehouse experience to your event — plus the full What's Shakin' menu is available too!

  • Cold brew, lattes, mochas & more
  • 10 signature specialty drinks
  • 27+ syrup flavors including sugar-free
  • Oat & almond milk options
  • Hot & iced drinks year-round
  • Full What's Shakin' menu also available
