FAQ

Common Questions

How far in advance should I book? +

We recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance, especially during peak season (May-September). However, we sometimes have last-minute availability, so don't hesitate to reach out!

Do you require a minimum guest count? +

Minimums vary depending on location and event type. Reach out and we'll work with you to find a package that fits!

Can I book both trucks for my event? +

Absolutely! Many of our clients book both What's Shakin' and What's Brewin' together. It's a great way to offer variety — refreshing shakes AND craft coffee for all your guests.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept cash, all major credit/debit cards, Venmo, and Cash App. We've got you covered!

Can I rent the lemon shake-up trailer for my wedding? +

Yes! Our vintage-style What's Shakin' trailer is a gorgeous addition to any wedding. We offer full-service options where we serve your guests, or rental-only options where we set up the trailer as a self-serve station or photo backdrop. Contact us for wedding packages and pricing!

Do you have sugar-free / dairy-free options? +

Yes! What's Shakin' has 9 sugar-free flavor options and stevia sweetener. What's Brewin' offers 7 sugar-free syrups plus oat and almond milk alternatives.
